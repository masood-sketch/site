Private Counsel.

A small number of individuals and families retain Dr Masood as private counsel - a trusted, confidential advisor on the decisions that shape a life and a family.

Some decisions are too personal for a consultant and too consequential for chance: a child's education and path into medicine, a health decision that needs an unhurried and independent mind, a family navigating a transition where trust matters more than any transaction.

These engagements are held in strict confidence, taken rarely, and shaped entirely around the people involved. There is no public calendar and no published detail. Where it is a fit, it begins with a private conversation.

The rarest thing is not advice. It is an advisor with no stake in the answer.
